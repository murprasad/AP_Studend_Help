>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I'm Murali, founder of StudentNest — an AP/SAT/ACT prep platform. We recently shipped a set of **focus-friendly study tools** for students who learn differently, and I think they may be a fit for your [resource directory / readers].
>
> They're built into the standard plan (no separate "ADHD tier," no medical claims) and include:
> - **Focus Mode** — a distraction-reduced, one-question-at-a-time study view
> - **Extended time** (1.5x / 2x) on practice and mock exams
> - **Energy check-ins** that suggest a session length based on the day
> - **Short-session design** + streak-forgiveness so one off day doesn't derail momentum
> - A 5-minute **study-style quiz** that routes each student to the right tools, and a **parent/coach progress view**
>
> We also published a free guide — *Study Strategies for Students Who Learn Differently* ([URL]) — that stands on its own regardless of whether anyone signs up.
>
> Would a short demo or a review copy be useful? Happy to tailor anything for your audience.
>
> Thanks for the work you do,
> Murali · StudentNest · [email] · [site]
